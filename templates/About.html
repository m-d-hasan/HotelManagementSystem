ABOUT Blue Parrot Resort

The Blue Parrot Resort at Basundhara, Dhaka offers an unmatched luxurious experience found nowhere else in Bangladesh. At the Palace you have world class accommodation to pick from. You can reside in the Main Tower Building. With either a fountain view or a garden view to choose from the rooms combine supreme comfort with modern amenities and easy access to all parts of the resort. For those who want to be even closer to nature the Villas are perfect for you providing you with all modern amenities in the most idyllic of settings with nature at your doorstep. Options available are the 1 Bedroom Villas, Honeymoon Villas, 2 Bedroom Villas, 3 Bedroom Villas and last but not least the Presidential Villas. The Presidential Villas offer unparalleled opulence over a vast area nestled in the heart of nature with state of the art amenities like a personal helipad, theater, indoor Jacuzzi, indoor steam bath, Smart 3D TVs and gaming consoles.
